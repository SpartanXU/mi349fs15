Social media is not real, says teen Instagram star

(CNN)Hundreds of likes on an Instagram picture of a perfectly posed, blond woman in a bikini may lead some to believe that she is confident and has a perfect life. But behind the scenes, Essena O'Neill felt like a lost and lonely teenager.That is what the social media personality wants her followers and the world of social media to know: What you see on social media isn't always true to life.
